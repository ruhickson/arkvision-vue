About the Creator

As a data analyst and software developer with personal experience living with glaucoma and uveitis, I understand firsthand the challenges of explaining visual conditions to others. This project was born from my desire to bridge the communication gap between patients, healthcare providers, and loved ones.

Professional Background

  • Data Analyst Specializing in healthcare data analysis and visualization
  • Software Developer Creating accessible and user-friendly applications
  • Patient Advocate Living with and managing glaucoma and uveitis

Why I Created This Tool

My journey with eye conditions began at the age of 7 when I developed my first cataract. Since then, I have experienced a litany of eye issues, including glaucoma and uveitis. These personal experiences have given me a deep understanding of the challenges faced by those with visual conditions.

Having experienced the difficulties of explaining my visual conditions to others, I recognized the need for a tool that could help people visualize and understand different eye conditions. This simulator aims to make it easier for patients to communicate their visual experiences and for others to better understand what living with these conditions means.

My hope is that this tool will help bridge the gap between patients and their support networks, making it easier to discuss visual conditions and their impact on daily life.
