Eye Care Resources in Ireland

Emergency Eye Care

  • Royal Victoria Eye and Ear Hospital
    Dublin's main eye emergency department. Open 24/7 for urgent eye care.
  • HSE Eye and Ear Hospital Services
    Directory of public eye care services across Ireland.

Professional Organizations

  • Optometry Ireland (OI)
    Professional representative body for over 800 practising Optometrists in Ireland, ensuring high standards in clinical and dispensing eye-care services.
  • Royal College of Surgeons Ireland - Ophthalmology
    Leading institution for ophthalmology education and research.
  • Irish College of Ophthalmologists
    Professional body for ophthalmologists in Ireland.

Support Organisations

  • Vision Ireland
    Leading national organisation providing services and support to people with sight loss in Ireland.
  • Fighting Blindness
    Leading patient-led charity funding research into treatments and cures for blindness.
  • Retina International
    Global network of patient organisations based in Dublin, focused on retinal degenerative diseases, research, and support.

Government Resources

  • HSE Primary Care Eye Team
    Information about public eye care services in Dublin South East and Wicklow, including paediatric eye assessments, appointments, and educational resources.
  • Citizens Information - Tax Reliefs for Visual Impairment
    Information about tax reliefs and financial supports available for people with visual impairment, including medical expenses and equipment.

Research and Education

  • National Optometry Centre (NOC) - TU Dublin
    State-of-the-art centre for optometry education, research, and specialist eye care services at TU Dublin Grangegorman.
  • Trinity College Dublin - Ophthalmology
    Ophthalmology education and research at Trinity College.
